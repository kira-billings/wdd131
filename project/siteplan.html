Site Purpose

To provide a personal, story-driven guide to commuting between St. George, Utah and Las Vegas, Nevada. It blends lived experience with practical tips, safety advice, and local insights. Throw in some pictures and stories to make it interesting.

Scenarios

Commuters and travelers who are curious about the desert drive between St. George, Utah and Las Vegas. What is the current weather? What are the best gas stations along the route? What dangers should I watch out for? When is the best time to travel? Personal stories about the commute and a photo gallery of amazing pictures.

Style Guide

Color Palette

Colors are soft muted desert colors for backgrounds. Dark text will be on the lighter tan background like shadows on the desert mountains. The plan is to make a simple ombre effect for the background with several colors to simulate the desert skies during dawn and dusk.

  • antique-white: rgb(251, 238, 218)
  • mint-cream: rgb(211, 237, 211)
  • light-cyan: rgb(200, 240, 249)
  • charcoal-blue: rgb(71, 75, 92)
  • evergreen: rgb(26, 52, 42)
  • amber: rgb(184, 132, 20)
  • link blue: rgb(22, 103, 168)

Typography

The font will be Google Font Overpass because it is similar to the road signs, simple, versatile, and easily read.

  • Headings: Overpass
  • Body Text: Overpass
